The faculty of medicine focuses on training healthcare professionals. Students study anatomy, physiology, pharmacology, and clinical practice to diagnose, treat, and prevent illnesses.

Faculty of Computing and Informatics

This faculty immerses students in technology, algorithms, and data. It equips learners with expertise in computer science, software development, and information systems.

Faculty of Business

The business faculty educates students in management, finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship. It prepares future leaders with the skills to navigate global markets and drive organizational success.

Faculty of Interdisciplinary

Offering a blend of diverse fields, this faculty integrates agriculture with other disciplines like environmental science, economics, and technology. Students explore sustainable farming practices, agribusiness management, and food security solutions to address complex agricultural challenges.

Faculty of Engineering

The engineering faculty focuses on equipping students with the knowledge and skills to innovate, design, and build solutions to real-world problems. Courses cover various engineering disciplines such as civil, mechanical, electrical, and chemical engineering. Students learn principles of mathematics, physics, and materials science alongside advanced engineering concepts to develop technologies ranging from infrastructure and machinery to electronics and renewable energy systems.

Faculty of Applied Sciences

This faculty merges scientific principles with practical applications. Students explore biology, chemistry, physics, and environmental science, focusing on hands-on experimentation and problem-solving. Graduates are equipped to address contemporary challenges in fields like renewable energy, biotechnology, and environmental conservation.
